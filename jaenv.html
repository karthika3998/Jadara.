Environmental Management Solutions

Environmental projects frequently call for rigorous analyses and a complete understanding of tested technology that can reduce both time and cost. A multidisciplinary team of environmental engineers has been assembled to provide cutting-edge solutions, delivering high-quality services in a variety of fields.

Environmental Compliance Audit

Assess the environmental performance and ensure compliance with laws and regulations.

Environmental Impact Assessment

Evaluate the effects of projects or developments on the environment to ensure sustainable decisions.

Environmental Site Assessment

Determine contamination risks in commercial and industrial properties before transactions.

Baseline Environmental Assessment

A Baseline Environmental Assessment (BEA) provides a snapshot of the environmental conditions of a property at the time of acquisition.

Environmental Noise & Vibration Monitoring

Monitor noise and vibrations to ensure worker health and compliance with local and international regulations.

Third Party Environmental Auditing

Conduct third-party audits to evaluate environmental management systems and compliance.

Marine Water Testing

Analyze seawater samples to assess water quality and detect potential hazards to human health and the environment.

Soil and Groundwater Testing

Test soil and groundwater for contaminants to assess risks to human health and the environment.

Noise Monitoring

Monitor noise levels to ensure compliance with regulations and safeguard workers' hearing health.

Ambient Temperature & Noise Monitoring

Monitor ambient temperature and noise to ensure safe working environments.

Site Assessment

Gather data to assess the risk of contamination on a site and ensure safe development.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensure that all operations and activities adhere to local, national, and international regulations.
